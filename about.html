Breanna Tam is a visual designer, who enjoys branding, illustrations, photography, and videography.

Hi hi, I’m Breanna (Bre) born and raised in Toronto, with a focus on visual design and photography. I am currently a Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo.

I am a passionate designer who seeks to change how users and audiences interact with my designs for the better. To me, design is the quickest way to share important information, in a more meaningful and gratifying way.

I continue to challenge myself through new learning experiences to expand my creative and technical skills. From past experience, I have gained several skills that have expanded my knowledge both in the creative and business industries. By following design systems, I have learned to be as expressive as possible, while only using brand guidelines. This allows me to express my skills with the company’s business vision in mind.
